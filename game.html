Pokemon Board Game

Game Phases

  • Player Movement: Starting from dealer chip, players can move.
  • Team Rocket Movement: Draw a team rocket card.
  • Events: Starting from dealer chip, players play their events.
  • Environment: Player with dealer chip draws an environment card.

Win Conditions

  • Beat Rival
  • Beat Giovanni
  • Beat 8 Gyms

Lose Conditions

  • Lose to Rival
  • Lose to Giovanni
  • Team Rocket Counter
  • Team Rocket Conquers 4 Gyms

Battle Rules

The phases are:

  • Items. If balls are used, skip the rest of the battle turn.
  • Defensive Sanity Check [5, 6]/ Offensive Sanity Check[4, 5, 6]
  • Attack/SpAttack Check [2, 3, 4, 5, 6]
  • Apply Statuses
    • Burn: Attack Check becomes [4, 5, 6]. Deal one damage during status phase.
    • Freeze: Speed becomes 0. SpAttack Check becomes [3, 4, 5, 6]. Do additional Freeze Check [4, 5, 6] after Sanity Check to move. Lasts 2 battle turns.
    • Paralyze: Speed is reducd by 5 (not below 1). Offensive Sanity Check becomes [5, 6]
    • Poison: SpAttack Check becomes [3, 4, 5, 6] and deal one damage during status phase.
