Pokemon Board Game

Link to game peripherals.

Player Starts

Game Phases

  • Player Movement: Starting from dealer chip, players can move up to 3 spaces. If you move to the same space occupied by a team rocket member, you must stop there.
  • Team Rocket Movement: Starting from dealer chip, each player draws a Team Rocket card. If a team rocket member moves to the same space as a player, the member stops moving.
  • Events: Starting from dealer chip, players play their events.
    • If you are standing on the same space as a team rocket member, you must fight him.
    • You may skip this event to fight a rare pokemon at a 50% chance if you are on a rare space, or fight a gym leader.
    • You must skip this step if you are knocked out. Heal all of your pokemon to full.
  • Environment: Player with dealer chip draws an environment card.

Pokemon Specifics

  • Single natural evolution pokemon evolve at level 4. Some require an evolution stone.
  • Double natural evolution pokemon evolve at level 3 and 5.
  • Pokemon must have 3 or less HP to have a chance of being captured.
  • Roll 4, 5 or 6 to capture regular Pokemon.
  • Roll 5 or 6 to capture rare Pokemon.
  • Roll 6 to capture legendary Pokemon.
  • Experience gained is equal to the level of the Pokemon defeated.
  • Getting knocked out causes you to lose your next encounter.
  • Staying in town allows you to fully heal one Pokemon. You can still encounter.

Win Conditions

  • Beat Giovanni. All players combine their pokemon and fight Giovanni together after 25 grunts are defeated.
  • Beat 8 Gyms. Gym leaders have two random level 4 pokemon. If a player owned pokemon is above level 4, gym leaders' pokemon are that level instead.

Lose Conditions

  • Lose to Giovanni.
  • Team Rocket have 25 grunts on board (raiders count as two, chieftains count as 3).
  • Team Rocket Conquers 4 Gyms (Gyms are captured when 3 grunts are on them).

Battle Rules

NPCs don't roll. Half and round up their stats for checks.

The phases are:

  • Items. If balls are used, skip the rest of the battle turn.
  • Speed Check [3, 4, 5, 6]
  • Attack/SpAttack Check [3, 4, 5, 6].
  • Apply Statuses
    • Burn: Attack Check becomes [5, 6].
    • Freeze: Roll a dice, with a 50% chance to skip next battle turn.
    • Paralyze: Speed is reduced to 0.
    • Poison: Deal three damage during status phase.
  • Go back to item step after this.
